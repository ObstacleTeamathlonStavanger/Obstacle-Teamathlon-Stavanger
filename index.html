Obstacle Teamathlon Stavanger

A fun and accessible mini-triathlon with a twist, bringing people together in Stavanger!

Event Overview

The Obstacle Teamathlon Stavanger is a team-based mini-triathlon with three unique categories:

  • Inflated Obstacle Water: A mix of swimming and climbing on inflatable obstacles.
  • Spinning Bikes: A relay-style "Mario Kart-inspired" route from Stavanger to Preikestolen (40 km).
  • Natural Obstacles Running Track: A 3 km track featuring natural obstacles like tree trunks and water crossings.

Key Features

This event uses digital displays to show the virtual position of teams in real-time, allowing participants to track their performance and share their progress on social media. The shorter distance and accessible format make it a fun and inclusive event for all fitness levels.

Event Details

  • Date: 22 June 2025
  • Start Time: 14:00 (approx. 3 hours duration)
  • Location: Stavanger, Norway
  • Teams: Groups of 6, fostering teamwork and fairness
  • Participant Goal: 10 teams of 6 (60 participants)

Mission & Vision

Mission: To be Stavanger's leading mini-triathlon event, encouraging community, fitness, and connection with nature.

Vision: Create a community event that welcomes everyone, emphasizing teamwork and meaningful connections.

Objectives

  • Attract 10 teams of 6 participants in the first year.
  • Provide a platform for 80% of participants to connect with new people to form teams.
  • Ensure at least 9 teams finish the race together.

Target Audience

Teams from companies, beginner triathletes, friends, sports associations, and fitness enthusiasts aged 18 to 45. Basic fitness is recommended, but the event is accessible to all.
